Commercial Project Process

We understand the importance of minimizing downtime for your business operations.

Phase 1: Planning & Assessment

Comprehensive site evaluation, traffic analysis, and chemical compatibility assessment. We develop a detailed project timeline to minimize business disruption.

Phase 2: Surface Preparation

Professional concrete preparation including shot blasting or diamond grinding, crack repair, and moisture mitigation. Proper preparation is critical for long-term performance.

Phase 3: Installation

Application of primer, base coat, and protective topcoat using commercial-grade equipment. We can work nights or weekends to accommodate your schedule.

Phase 4: Quality Control & Handover

Comprehensive inspection, performance testing, and documentation. We provide maintenance guidelines and warranty information.
