Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Epoxy Flooring

How long does epoxy flooring last?

Professional epoxy flooring typically lasts 15-20 years with proper maintenance. Our lifetime warranty covers peeling and delamination, ensuring your investment is protected.

What's the cost of garage epoxy flooring in North Jersey?

Garage epoxy flooring costs between $3-8 per square foot depending on the system chosen. A typical 2-car garage (400 sq ft) ranges from $1,200-3,200 including surface preparation.

How long does installation take?

Most residential garage installations take 2-3 days. Day 1: surface preparation, Day 2: epoxy application, Day 3: topcoat. You can walk on it after 24 hours, drive on it after 72 hours.

Can epoxy be installed over cracked concrete?

Yes, we repair all cracks and imperfections during surface preparation. Our process includes crack filling, leveling, and moisture mitigation to ensure a perfect substrate.

Is epoxy flooring slippery when wet?

We add anti-slip additives to all our epoxy systems, creating a textured surface that's safe even when wet. This is especially important for North Jersey's wet winters.
